# MatchMySkillset - Innovative Feature Ideas
## Compiled April 2026

---

# 1. SKILL TRANSLATOR ENGINE
User describes experience in plain English ("I organised a charity fundraiser for 200 people"). AI maps to formal skills with confidence scores: Event Management (92%), Budget Management (88%), Team Leadership (85%). Generates employer-facing CV language. Uses O*NET + ESCO as base. Tracks "skill velocity" (which skills gaining/losing demand in real time).

**Gap it fills:** Most taxonomies are static. Nobody translates informal/life experience to formal skill language.

---

# 2. CAREER GPS (Multiple Routes)
Like Google Maps showing 3 routes:
- **Express Route:** Fastest path to target role, intensive upskilling
- **Scenic Route:** Stepping-stone roles, gradual, lower risk
- **Wildcard Route:** Unexpected paths others with similar profiles took

Each shows: timeline, salary trajectory, required upskilling, probability of success.
Plus "What if" simulator: "What if I spent 3 months learning Python?" shows how paths change.

**Gap it fills:** Existing tools show average paths. None show personalised paths based on your unique skills/constraints.

---

# 3. VERIFIED POTENTIAL PROFILES (Reverse Recruitment for Career Changers)
- Candidates complete structured skill demonstrations (mini-projects, scenarios)
- Platform scores on POTENTIAL, not just experience
- Blind matching phase: employers review anonymised skill profiles first (no name, photo, previous titles)
- Only after expressing interest do they see full profile
- Reduces bias against career changers

**Gap it fills:** Reverse recruitment only works for in-demand tech talent. Nobody's cracked it for career changers.

---

# 4. GAP CLOSER DASHBOARD
User selects target job. AI analyses gap at three levels:
- **Hard skills:** "You need SQL. 40-hour course, free on freeCodeCamp, or 8-hour intensive on Coursera for £39"
- **Experience gaps:** "You need client-facing experience. Here are 3 volunteer roles and 2 freelance gigs"
- **Credential gaps:** "This role typically requires X cert. Here's how to get it in 6 weeks"

Progress tracking: match score updates in real time as users complete learning.
Cost-optimised: always shows free option alongside paid.
Time-to-qualified estimate: "At 10 hours/week, qualified in 14 weeks."

**Gap it fills:** Nobody provides precise, personalised gap analysis for specific jobs with costed training paths.

---

# 5. CAREER CHANGE FINANCIAL PLANNER
- Salary trajectory: current path vs each new path (1, 3, 5, 10 years)
- Factors in: retraining costs, salary dip during transition, growth rate
- Break-even calculator: "Recoup dip and training costs in 2.3 years"
- Regional adjustment: same role, different salary London vs Manchester vs remote
- Hidden compensation: benefits, pension, stock options, work-life balance value
- Negotiation insights: typical range for this role at this company

**Gap it fills:** Salary data exists but disconnected from career transition context. Nobody shows total financial impact.

---

# 6. SKILL PROOF SYSTEM
- **Micro-challenges:** 15-30 min scenario-based assessments simulating real work
  - "A client emails complaining about a delayed project. Draft your response."
  - "Here's a dataset. What are the 3 most important insights?"
- AI-scored with human review for calibration
- Portable: shareable link, embeddable badge, verifiable by employers
- **Specific peer endorsements:** Not "John is great at leadership" but "John led our 6-person volunteer team for 8 months and delivered the event on time"
- **Video introductions:** 60-second structured video answering a scenario question

**Gap it fills:** No universal, cross-industry way to prove soft skills, leadership, or domain knowledge outside traditional credentials.

---

# 7. TRANSITION TWINS (Mentor Matching)
Database of people who successfully changed careers, tagged with: origin industry, destination, skills leveraged, time taken, challenges, regrets.

AI matches you with someone who made the exact same transition.

**6-session structured programme:**
1. "Is this right for me?" (Reality check)
2. "What do I already have?" (Skills audit)
3. "What's the gap?" (Honest assessment)
4. "How did you bridge it?" (Practical tactics)
5. "Getting the first role" (Application strategy)
6. "First 90 days" (Surviving the transition)

**Group cohorts:** 5-8 people making the same transition, with a mentor, meeting weekly for 8 weeks.

**Gap it fills:** Nobody matches career changers with people who made the exact same move.

---

# 8. REALITY CHECK (Industry Insider Insights)
Structured interviews with real professionals:
- Typical day/week breakdown (actual time allocations)
- Best and worst parts (with specifics)
- What surprised them most
- Skills that matter most (vs what job ads say)
- Salary reality (early, mid, senior career)
- Advice for career changers entering this field

Verified by employment. Video snippets. Anonymous Q&A.
**"Shadow Day" marketplace:** Connect career changers with professionals willing to let them observe.

**Gap it fills:** Glassdoor reviews are about companies, not roles. "Day in life" content is performative.

---

# 9. CREDENTIAL EFFECTIVENESS SCORE
Track which credentials actually lead to job offers (anonymised, with consent).
Rate every course/cert/bootcamp on:
- **Hire rate** (% of completers who got relevant jobs)
- **Time to hire**
- **Salary impact**
- **Employer recognition score**

"TripAdvisor for upskilling."

**Curated "career change packs":** For each transition (teacher to UX), a bundle of 3-5 credentials that make you hireable.
**Employer-endorsed learning paths:** Companies specify "complete X, Y, Z and we will interview you." Guaranteed interview.

**Gap it fills:** Micro-credential landscape is fragmented. Nobody knows which ones actually help you get hired.

---

# 10. TRANSITION COHORTS (Community)
Time-bound groups (8-12 weeks) of 10-15 people making similar transitions.
- Weekly themes (Skills audit, Target roles, CV overhaul, etc.)
- Accountability partners
- Milestone celebrations
- Alumni network: "Class of Q2 2026, Teacher to Tech"
- Anonymity option (fear of current employer)
- Async-first (for people with jobs, caring responsibilities)

**Gap it fills:** Career changers feel isolated. Leaving professional identity and community with nothing to replace it.

---

# 11. AI + HUMAN CHECKPOINT MODEL
AI handles: skills matching, gap analysis, job discovery, application tracking, learning recommendations.
Human recruiter intervenes at 3 critical moments:
1. **Career direction validation** (30 min): "AI suggests these 5 paths. Let me pressure-test that."
2. **Application review** (15 min per app): "For this company, emphasise X, remove Y."
3. **Offer negotiation support** (30 min): "Here's what to ask for, here's how to frame it."

Specialist recruiters per transition type (military to civilian, creative to corporate, returning parents).
Quality guarantee: follow the plan, no interviews in 90 days = full refund.

---

# 12. CAREER CURIOSITY CONTENT FUNNEL (Passive Engagement)
Content-first: "5 Signs You've Outgrown Teaching" (SEO-optimised, genuinely helpful).
"Career Temperature" check: 3-minute non-threatening assessment.
Results (with consent) create warm candidate pool.
Monthly drip: "People with your skills are being hired for X at Y% higher salary."
When ready, all previous engagement already in the platform. Zero friction to activate.

**Gap it fills:** All passive engagement targets people already in target role. Nobody engages potential career changers who haven't realised they could switch.

---

# 13. WORK DNA (Culture Matching)
Both candidates and teams complete same assessment:
- Work style, values, communication, growth preferences
- Match score shows compatibility AND complementarity
- "Culture preview" videos from actual team members
- Post-hire tracking to validate predictions

**Gap it fills:** "Culture fit" often justifies bias. Nobody measures what a career changer ADDS.

---

# 14. TRANSITION GIGS
Short-term (1-4 week) paid projects for career changers to gain experience.
- Discounted rates (candidate accepts lower pay for experience)
- Structured like apprenticeship: mentor, learning outcomes, skills endorsement
- Portfolio builder: each gig adds verified entry to profile
- Bridge to permanent: convert to hire (platform takes placement fee)
- "Try Before You Buy" for employers: lowest-risk way to hire a career changer

**Gap it fills:** Nobody positions freelancing as a career transition tool. Platforms assume you're already a freelancer.

---

# 15. RELAUNCH PAD (Return to Work)
- Skills refresh: "Here's what changed while you were away"
- Confidence rebuilding programme
- Flexible-first matching: every role tagged with genuine flexibility details
- Returnship creation tool for SMEs (templates, mentoring frameworks, co-funding via government grants)
- "Career Break Positive" employer badge (like Disability Confident scheme)

**Gap it fills:** Returnship programmes exist only at large corporates. SMEs (60% of UK workforce) have nothing.

# PART 3: FRED'S ROLE (RECRUITMENT AGENT CO-FOUNDER)

Fred's recruitment background is arguably the single biggest competitive advantage MMS has. Here's how to monetise it:

## 1. Direct Placement Fees
Fred's existing network of employers and candidates is immediately monetisable.

**How it works:**
- Fred continues making placements using MMS as his matching tool
- Standard UK recruitment fees: 15-20% of first-year salary
- Average UK salary for career changers' target roles: ~£35,000-50,000
- Average fee per placement: £5,250-10,000

**Revenue potential:**
- 2 placements/month = £10,000-20,000/month
- This alone could fund the platform's early development
- Fred essentially becomes the platform's first "power user" and case study

**Key point:** Fred's placements prove the matching algorithm works and generate testimonials/case studies for marketing.

---

## 2. Employer Partnership Development
Fred's contacts are the fastest path to solving the chicken-and-egg problem.

**Actions:**
- Onboard 20-50 employers from his network in Month 1
- Offer them free/discounted access in exchange for job listings and feedback
- Each employer relationship is worth £2,400-12,000/year in subscriptions once proven
- Fred becomes VP of Employer Partnerships / Head of Business Development

**Revenue potential:**
- 30 employer partnerships x £300/month avg = £9,000/month within 6 months
- Growing to 100+ employers by Year 2

---

## 3. SME Hiring Consultancy
Most SMEs (under 50 employees) don't have HR departments and struggle with hiring.

**Service offering:**
- "AI-Assisted Hiring Audit" - £500 per engagement

- Job description optimisation using MMS data - £100 per JD
- Includes MMS platform access as part of the package

**Revenue potential:**
- 5 SME clients x £750/month = £3,750/month
- Scales to £10,000+/month with dedicated team

## 4. Training Other Recruiters
Fred can build a paid programme teaching recruiters how to use AI tools effectively.

**Revenue potential:**
- 2 workshops/month x £3,000 = £6,000/month

- Annual: ~£100,000/year
